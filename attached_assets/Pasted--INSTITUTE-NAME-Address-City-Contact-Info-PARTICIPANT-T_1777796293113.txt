TERMS & CONDITIONS
1. Photo, Video & Promotional Content Consent
I grant the institute permission to photograph and/or video-record my participation in classes, rehearsals, and performances. I consent to the use of this material for the institute’s promotional purposes, including but not limited to social media posts, reels, website content, and printed material. I understand that:
a) I will not be compensated for the use of such content.
b) The institute will make reasonable efforts to use content tastefully and respectfully.
c) I may request removal of specific content by written notice, and the institute will make reasonable efforts to comply.
2. Expenses & Financial Responsibility
I understand and agree that the following expenses are my personal responsibility and are not covered by the institute:
a) Costumes, accessories, jewellery, and makeup for performances.
b) Travel and accommodation costs related to rehearsals or events.
c) Any other expense communicated by the teacher/institute in advance.
I also understand the fee structure for the program as communicated at the time of enrollment, and that fees once paid are subject to the refund policy outlined in Clause 6.
3. Event / Performance Participation
I confirm my interest and willingness to participate in the annual event/performance organized by the institute. I understand that participation is subject to:
a) Meeting the minimum attendance and rehearsal requirements (see Clause 5).
b) Adhering to the creative direction of the teacher (see Clause 4).
c) The institute’s final discretion on casting, formations, and performance assignments.
4. Creative Direction & Choreography
I agree to follow the choreography, formations, styling, and creative direction as decided by the teacher/choreographer. I understand that:
a) Individual modifications or deviations are not permitted without prior approval from the teacher.
b) The choreography taught is the intellectual property of the teacher/institute and may not be reproduced, taught, or shared publicly (including on social media) without written permission.
5. Attendance & Rehearsal Commitment
I commit to attending all scheduled classes and rehearsals as communicated by the institute. I understand that:
a) A minimum attendance of ____% is required to remain eligible for the performance.
b) Absences must be communicated to the teacher in advance (not through other participants).
c) If my attendance falls below the required threshold, the institute reserves the right to reassign my role or remove me from the performance item, without refund of fees.
6. Withdrawal & Refund Policy
I understand the following withdrawal terms:
a) Withdrawal on or before [DATE] – [full/partial] refund of program fees.
b) Withdrawal after [DATE] – no refund, as costs will have been committed by the institute.
c) In the event of withdrawal, I remain bound by the intellectual property and confidentiality terms of this agreement.
7. Code of Conduct
I agree to conduct myself respectfully toward the teacher, fellow participants, and any event staff or volunteers. I understand that:
a) Disruptive, disrespectful, or inappropriate behaviour may result in removal from the program without refund.
b) Conflicts or concerns should be raised directly with the teacher or institute management, not discussed publicly or on social media.
8. Communication Protocol
I agree to communicate any absences, concerns, scheduling conflicts, or issues directly to the teacher/institute through the designated communication channel (e.g., phone, email, or specified group). I will not rely on informal or third-party communication.
9. Social Media & Confidentiality
I understand the following regarding social media and confidentiality:
a) I will not post rehearsal videos, behind-the-scenes content, or choreography clips on any personal or public platform without the institute’s prior written approval.
b) Internal feedback, corrections, group dynamics, or any disputes are confidential and must not be shared publicly.
c) Positive mentions and event promotions are encouraged, in alignment with the institute’s branding guidelines.

I participate at my own risk and agree that the institute, its teachers, and staff are not liable for any injuries, accidents, or health issues arising from participation in classes, rehearsals, or performances.
